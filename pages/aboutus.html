About Us

Job that fits student's life

At UniTalent! Our platform is dedicated to helping students find the perfect job opportunities to kickstart their careers. Whether you’re looking for part-time work, internships, or entry-level positions, UniTalent connects you with employers who value your potential. Create your profile, explore tailored job listings, and apply effortlessly. Employers can post student-focused roles and discover talented individuals eager to grow. At UniTalent, we’re bridging the gap between education and professional success!

Frequently Asked Questions

01 Can I upload a CV? +

Of course! To increase your successful rate, we do recommend to upload your CV along with your application. This can help the recruiter getting you more information about you.

02 How long will the recruitment process take? +

The recruitment process typically takes 1-2 weeks depending on the role.

03 Do you recruit for Graduates, Ausbildung and Students +

Yes, we specialize in connecting students, recent graduates, and individuals undergoing Ausbildung (vocational training) with exciting job opportunities. Whether you’re looking for internships, part-time work, or entry-level positions, UniTalent is here to support you in finding the right role that matches your skills and aspirations.

04 What does the recruitment and selection process involve? +

The recruitment and selection process typically involves the following steps:

  1. Job Search & Application: Browse available job listings on UniTalent and apply to positions that interest you.
  2. Screening: Employers review your application and profile to match their requirements.
  3. Interview: If shortlisted, you may be invited for an interview (virtual or in-person).
  4. Decision: Employers will notify you about their final decision, and successful candidates will receive further instructions on the next steps.

We aim to ensure a smooth and transparent process for both job seekers and employers.

05 Can I receive notification for any future jobs that may interest me? +

Yes, you can! By creating an account and setting up job alerts, you will receive notifications about new job opportunities that match your preferences. Simply select your areas of interest, and we’ll keep you updated via email or through your UniTalent dashboard whenever a relevant position is posted.
